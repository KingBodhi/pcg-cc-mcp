## Overview

This playbook extends the PCG behavioral testing framework to focus on executive leaders who rely on the Nora agent to orchestrate mixed human/AI tasking. Each test spotlights how a CEO initiates delegation, how Nora splits responsibilities across human specialists and AI automations, and which signals confirm client expectations are met. The suite is designed for behavioral load testing, workflow instrumentation, and qualitative review sessions.

## Client Expectations Summary

### WillRise Safety Systems (Physical Product E-commerce)
- Risk-averse engineering CEO prioritises regulatory compliance, quality assurance, and audit trails.
- Expects Nora to uphold professional tone, surface compliance checkpoints, and keep humans in the loop for approvals.
- Values predictable delivery timelines, thorough documentation, and transparent escalation paths.

### DecentralDAO Collective (DAO Project)
- Crypto-native CEO emphasises transparency, community trust, and distributed decision making.
- Wants Nora to respect on-chain governance rituals, coordinate global contributors, and highlight security risks early.
- Success is measured in participation rates, policy adherence, and accurate treasury visibility.

### QuestMaster Games (Hybrid Gaming Studio)
- Creative CEO balances physical production with live digital operations and community storytelling.
- Relies on Nora for cross-media coordination, player safety guardrails, and hybrid human/AI content production.
- Looks for high-quality fan engagement, consistent release cadence, and inclusive community management.
